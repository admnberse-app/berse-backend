# Offerings - User Journey Documentation

## Overview
The Offerings feature allows Berse users to showcase and discover services they provide to the community. This includes Local Guide services, Homestay accommodations, Marketplace offerings, networking opportunities (Open to Connect), activity partnerships (BerseBuddy), and mentoring services (BerseMentor). The feature creates a comprehensive service ecosystem within the Berse trust network.

## User Personas

### Primary Users

#### 1. Service Providers
- **Local Guides**: Experienced locals who can show travelers around their city
- **Homestay Hosts**: Users offering accommodation in their homes
- **Marketplace Vendors**: Users selling products or services
- **Mentors**: Professionals offering expertise in their field
- **Activity Partners**: Users seeking companions for activities

#### 2. Service Seekers
- **Travelers**: Looking for local guidance and authentic experiences
- **Accommodation Seekers**: Needing trusted homestay options
- **Buyers**: Seeking products or services within the community
- **Mentees**: Looking for professional guidance and learning
- **Activity Enthusiasts**: Wanting companions for shared interests

## User Journey Map

### Phase 1: Discovery & Motivation

#### Trigger Events
- User completes profile setup
- User views other members' service offerings
- User has a specific service need (travel, accommodation, etc.)
- Profile completion prompts suggest adding services
- User receives inquiry about services they might offer

#### User Goals
- **Service Providers**: Monetize skills, help community members, build reputation
- **Service Seekers**: Find trusted services within the community, authentic experiences

### Phase 2: Service Provider Journey

#### Step 1: Service Discovery & Decision
**User Actions:**
- Browse existing service categories
- Consider personal skills and availability
- Evaluate potential demand in their area
- Review other providers' offerings for inspiration

**System Response:**
- Display service category overview with examples
- Show local demand indicators
- Provide service setup guidance
- Display potential earnings/benefits information

**User Emotions:** Curiosity, opportunity recognition, slight hesitation

#### Step 2: Service Setup
**User Actions:**
- Navigate to profile or offerings section
- Select service categories to offer
- Toggle service availability on/off
- Add service-specific details and descriptions

**System Response:**
- Present service selection interface with clear icons and descriptions
- Allow multiple service selection
- Provide guidance for each service type
- Save selections and update profile

**User Emotions:** Excitement, commitment, anticipation

#### Step 3: Service Details Configuration
**User Actions:**
- Add detailed service descriptions
- Set availability and pricing (if applicable)
- Upload relevant photos or portfolio items
- Configure service-specific settings

**System Response:**
- Provide service-specific forms and fields
- Allow rich text descriptions
- Support media uploads
- Validate required information

**User Emotions:** Professional pride, attention to detail

#### Step 4: Profile Integration
**User Actions:**
- Review how services appear on profile
- Adjust service visibility and privacy settings
- Preview profile as potential customers would see it

**System Response:**
- Display real-time profile preview
- Show service badges and indicators
- Integrate services with trust chain display
- Update profile completeness score

**User Emotions:** Satisfaction, professional presentation

## Service-Specific User Journeys

### Local Guide Service Journey

#### Provider Setup
1. **Expertise Declaration**: User indicates local knowledge areas
2. **Availability Setting**: Define when and how long tours are available
3. **Specialization Areas**: Cultural sites, food tours, hidden gems, etc.
4. **Language Capabilities**: What languages they can guide in

#### Seeker Experience
1. **Destination Planning**: User planning travel to specific location
2. **Guide Discovery**: Search for local guides in destination
3. **Experience Selection**: Choose guide based on specialties and interests
4. **Tour Coordination**: Plan itinerary and meeting arrangements
5. **Experience Delivery**: Receive personalized local guidance

### Homestay Service Journey

#### Provider Setup
1. **Accommodation Details**: Space type, amenities, house rules
2. **Availability Calendar**: When space is available
3. **Pricing Structure**: Rates and payment terms
4. **Guest Requirements**: Preferred guest types and requirements

#### Seeker Experience
1. **Accommodation Search**: Need for trusted, local accommodation
2. **Homestay Discovery**: Browse available homestays in area
3. **Host Evaluation**: Review host profiles and accommodation details
4. **Booking Process**: Reserve dates and confirm arrangements
5. **Stay Experience**: Experience local hospitality and culture

### Marketplace Service Journey

#### Provider Setup
1. **Product/Service Catalog**: What they're selling or offering
2. **Pricing and Terms**: Cost structure and transaction terms
3. **Delivery Options**: How products/services are delivered
4. **Business Verification**: Credentials and business legitimacy

#### Seeker Experience
1. **Product/Service Need**: Specific need for goods or services
2. **Marketplace Browse**: Explore available offerings
3. **Vendor Evaluation**: Review seller profiles and offerings
4. **Purchase Process**: Transaction and delivery coordination
5. **Product/Service Receipt**: Receive and evaluate purchase

## Pain Points & Solutions

### Common Pain Points

#### For Service Providers
1. **Low Visibility**: Services not being discovered by potential customers
   - *Solution*: Enhanced search algorithms, featured provider programs

2. **Trust Building**: Difficulty establishing credibility with new customers
   - *Solution*: Integration with trust chain, verification badges, review systems

3. **Service Management**: Tracking availability, bookings, and communications
   - *Solution*: Integrated service management tools, calendar integration

4. **Pricing Complexity**: Determining fair pricing for services
   - *Solution*: Market rate suggestions, community pricing insights

#### For Service Seekers
1. **Quality Uncertainty**: Not knowing if service provider is reliable
   - *Solution*: Trust chain integration, verified reviews, community feedback

2. **Communication Barriers**: Difficulty coordinating with service providers
   - *Solution*: In-app messaging, translation tools, coordination templates

3. **Limited Options**: Not enough service providers in desired area/category
   - *Solution*: Geographic expansion incentives, referral programs

4. **Booking Complexity**: Complicated process to secure services
   - *Solution*: Streamlined booking flows, automated confirmations

### Technical Pain Points

1. **Search Performance**: Slow or inaccurate service search results
   - *Solution*: Optimized search indexing, location-based filtering

2. **Mobile Experience**: Poor mobile interface for service management
   - *Solution*: Mobile-first design, responsive service interfaces

3. **Payment Processing**: Complex or insecure payment handling
   - *Solution*: Integrated payment solutions, secure transaction processing
